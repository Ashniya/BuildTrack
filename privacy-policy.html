Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At BuildTrack, we believe transparency builds trust. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information as you help build the future with our platform. We're committed to keeping your construction data secure while delivering powerful project insights.

1 Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information

  • Contact details (name, email, phone)
  • Company information
  • Payment details (for premium services)

Project Data

  • Construction progress updates
  • Site photos and videos
  • Project timelines and milestones

We only collect what's necessary to deliver our services effectively.

2 How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us create a seamless construction management experience:

  • Provide real-time project tracking
  • Generate automated progress reports
  • Improve our platform features
  • Respond to your inquiries

We never use your data for purposes you haven't consented to.

3 Data Security Measures

We protect your construction data like we protect our own buildings - with multiple layers of security:

Encryption

All data encrypted in transit and at rest

Access Control

Role-based permissions for team members

Backups

Regular automated backups

We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing.

4 Your Rights & Choices

You're in control of your data. As a BuildTrack user, you can:

  • A Access your personal information we hold
  • B Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • C Delete your account and associated data
  • D Export your project data for your records

To exercise these rights, please contact our support team.

5 Policy Updates

We may update this policy as we add new features or respond to legal requirements:

We'll notify you about significant changes via email or in-app notification at least 30 days before they take effect.

Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
